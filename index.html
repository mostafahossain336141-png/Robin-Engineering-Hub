Testing Instruments Handled

  • OMICRON CMC 356 — universal relay test set for protection relay testing, commissioning, and functional verification
  • OMICRON CPC 100 — universal primary injection test system for testing power transformers, CTs, VTs, circuit breakers, and other primary equipment
  • SMRT Relay Test Set — secondary injection testing and protection relay commissioning
  • MRCT (Magnetization & Ratio CT Tester) — current transformer ratio, polarity, excitation, burden, and winding resistance testing

  • DILO SF₆ Gas Analyzer — SF₆ gas quality analysis including purity, moisture, and decomposition products
